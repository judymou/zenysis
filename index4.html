  • Data integration and analysis platform

    Our integration software helps governments view and analyze all their data in one place. When governments face pandemic emergencies, humanitarian crises, or natural disasters, they turn to their data for actionable insights. Zenysis puts this information at their fingertips.

  • BUILT FOR DEVELOPING COUNTRIES

    We are not another contractor or prepackaged system. Our software is developed with direct input from our users on the ground and tested on their machines. For the first time, developing countries have software that is suited to their needs.

  • Global impact with startup technologies

    Technology has done great things for developed countries, but there are 150 governments and 6 billion people whose quality of life can be vastly improved by the power of software. Zenysis brings startup technology, speed, and execution to serve governments and populations that are historically underserved.
